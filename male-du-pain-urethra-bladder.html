Bladder or Urethral Infection (Urethritis)

Pain during urination and in the urethra is the HALLMARK symptom of bladder or urethral infection in men and women. Urethral infections are often called "urethritis" but bladder infections can also cause urethral burning.

ACTION STEP - The first step is to rule out a simple bladder infection.

CAUTION - Please contact your physician if you have any alarming symptoms and/or if they don't improve within a reasonable amount of time. Bacterial infections can be notoriously difficult to treat due to the rise in antibiotic resistent infections.
