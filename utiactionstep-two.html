Step Two - Treatment

If infection is found via culture (no guessing please), the laboratory will normally then do some antibiotic sensitivity testing to determine which antibiotic is most effective against your specific infection. Your physician will recommend an antibiotic based upon those results. Please don't self-medicate with an old antibiotic at home.

CAUTION - Please contact your physician if you have any alarming symptoms and/or if they don't improve within a reasonable amount of time. Bacterial infections can be notoriously difficult to treat due to the rise in antibiotic resistent infections.
