Rescue Plan: 0 - 30 Minutes

  • Rest - If you've been in pain or discomfort for just thirty minutes, stop what you are doing, sit down, rest and use a heating pad for several minutes. Our goal here to break the cycle of irritation and/or muscle tension before it worsens.
  • Heat - A heating pad can help relax your muscles and prevent additional muscle tension from developing.
