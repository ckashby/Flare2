Men as they age often develop benign prostate enlargement. When the prostate becomes enlarged, it can reduce and block urine flow. Other symptoms of BPH include:

  • urinary hesitancy
  • starting and stopping of urination
  • weak urine stream
  • dribbling of urine
  • urgency
  • difficulty sleeping
  • straining to urinate
  • frequent UTI
  • bladder stones

ACTION STEP - If you struggle with these symptoms, please contact your physician. They'll help determine to what degree, if any, your prostate is involved.

CAUTION - Please contact your physician if you have any alarming symptoms and/or if they don't improve within a reasonable amount of time. Bacterial infections can be notoriously difficult to treat due to the rise in antibiotic resistent infections.
